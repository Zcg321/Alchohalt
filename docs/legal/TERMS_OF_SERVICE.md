## 3. The app is NOT medical advice and NOT an emergency service

Alchohalt is a **personal tracking tool**. Nothing in the app — drink
log entries, streak counters, money-saved totals, journal prompts,
mood-tag analytics, or any AI-assisted insight feature we may release
in the future — constitutes medical, psychiatric, addiction-treatment,
or mental-health advice.

If you are experiencing:

- Suicidal thoughts → call **988** (US) or your country's equivalent
  immediately. The in-app Crisis Resources page links 988, the SAMHSA
  helpline (1-800-662-HELP), Alcoholics Anonymous, and SMART Recovery.
  Those resources are NEVER gated behind any paywall.
- Acute alcohol withdrawal symptoms (tremors, hallucinations, seizure
  risk) → call **911** or your local emergency number. Alcohol
  withdrawal can be fatal; do not attempt to manage it through an app.
- A medical emergency of any kind → call your local emergency number.

**We are not your doctor, therapist, sponsor, or recovery counselor.**
The app does not replace any of those relationships, and the developer
is not licensed to practice medicine.
