## Round summary

### What landed

- **R15-A** Tag explorer surface: tap any tag in Tag Patterns to see
  weekday distribution, hour-of-day distribution, last 10 entries.
  Inline expand-in-place; aria-expanded; close button.
- **R15-B** First active A/B test: onboarding chip copy. Control
  ("Trying to drink less") vs first-person ("I want to drink less").
  Deterministic per-device assignment via R14-4 scaffold; exposures
  recorded locally; intent IDs stay stable across variants so
  diagnostics remain consistent.
- **R15-C** Std-drink jurisdiction callout in Diagnostics. New
  locale-derived default for users who haven't picked one
  explicitly — `en-GB` → `uk`, `fr-FR` → `eu`, etc. Detected default
  is NOT persisted (only an explicit user choice writes to settings).
  Deep-link to the SettingsPanel picker.
- **R15-1** Monthly delta extension: drinking-days + avg-per-drinking-day
  with month-over-month percent deltas. Surfaces session-intensity
  changes that AF-day-count alone hides.
- **R15-2** Goal nudges (calm, opt-in). Trailing-7-day avg vs daily-cap
  comparison; in-app banner only (no system notifications); one-tap
  revisit, one-tap dismiss-for-week. Off by default.
- **R15-3** Backup auto-verification. Every export round-trips through
  `validateImport` immediately; result persisted; small ribbon at top
  of app on failure with deep-link to Diagnostics.
- **R15-4** Printable Trust Receipt. Pure HTML builder; Save/print
  button in the Trust Receipt panel. Per-event redaction guarantee
  carries through; @media print rules tighten pagination.
- **R15-5** Designer-of-competing-app judge. Stress-test of R14's
  competitor-PM moat answer. Conclusion: feature-to-copy is the
  printable receipt artifact (cheap, high marketing leverage);
  feature-impossible-to-copy is the no-server architecture.
- **R15-6** Fifteen-judge spectacular gate. 90 ship verdicts, 9
  critical-pass, 2 D-entries logged. Verdict: ship.
