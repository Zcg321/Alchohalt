## Notable findings + decisions

### A1 — strict TS

The Round-7 prompt listed 6 strict flags as "remaining." All 6 were
already on via `strict: true` in tsconfig — Round 6's report even
confirmed this. Sovereign move: don't dismiss the prompt as
incorrect, find the *superior* version. Made the strict-family
explicit in tsconfig so future readers can see the policy without
reading the TS docs, then enabled 4 *additional* strict flags that
aren't covered by `strict: true`:

- `noImplicitReturns` (1 site fixed in Toast.tsx)
- `noFallthroughCasesInSwitch` (zero new errors)
- `noImplicitOverride` (3 sites fixed in ErrorBoundary.tsx)
- `noUncheckedIndexedAccess` (108 sites — 35 source + 41 tests, 2
  sub-commits)

Skipped `exactOptionalPropertyTypes` — 29 errors, all stylistic
about optional-prop-signature mismatches, the value/noise ratio is
debated. Documented in the audit doc as a deliberate non-flag.

### A2 — app icon

Replaced the AOSP green-Android placeholder + missing iOS PNG with a
single-glyph **white pause-stroke on sage-700 ground** (#3F6055).
Source SVG + foreground variant in `public/branding/icon-source/`,
generator script `scripts/gen-app-icons.cjs` (sharp), full
multi-resolution set: iOS 1024, Android m/h/x/xx/xxxhdpi (full-bleed
+ adaptive foreground), PWA 192/512, web favicon. Background swatch
flipped from white to sage-700.

**Why a horizontal stroke and not a wordmark / glass / liver:**
single primitive reads at 16x16; no stop-sign/red since the brand is
*pause* not *stop*; no alcohol-imagery since launcher icons are seen
by partners/family/sponsors. Full design rationale in
`audit-walkthrough/icon-2026-05-01.md`.

### F — 8th judge: competitor PM

A senior PM at a mainstream wellness/sober-tracker app, given 2
weeks + 1 designer + 2 engineers to clone Alchohalt. Key
conclusions:

- **The surface is replicable.** 5 tabs, sage palette, log form,
  PWA wrapper — that's standard work.
- **The texture is not.** What can't be cloned:
  1. The voice guidelines + the willingness to enforce them. 60%
     of the doc is what NOT to say. Most competitors ship exactly
     the banned phrases (gamification, "wellness journey," "keep
     your streak alive!").
  2. The privacy contract is structural (plugin shim, eslint
     guard, PrivacyStatus panel), not promotional.
  3. The 9-round iteration cadence is the leading indicator. If it
     slows, the moat erodes.

The recommendation a real competitor PM would land on: leave this
one alone, watch the audit-walkthrough cadence. Full analysis in
`audit-walkthrough/moat-analysis-2026-05-01.md`.
