### A1 — Wellness dashboard copy

`[WELLNESS-COPY-ROUND-4]` cec4959

Three tiles had label/value mismatches that round 1+2+3 missed.
"Average craving: 80%" computed `(5 - avgCraving) * 20` — a reader
parses 80% as "craving is high" when the math meant the inverse.
"Evening drinking: 76/100" and "HALT-tagged drinks: 65/100" had
the same shape: behaviour-named tile, derived 0–100 score.

Each tile now shows the raw count or average it's named after:

| Tile | Was | Now |
|------|-----|-----|
| Average craving | `(5 - avgCraving) * 20`% | `avgCraving.toFixed(1)` / 5 |
| Evening drinking | sleepScore /100 | `eveningCount` in 30 days |
| HALT-tagged drinks | `100 - stressLevel` /100 | `haltTaggedCount` in 30 days |
| Social with alternatives | % | `% of N` (or — when N=0) |

Empty states render `—` instead of dividing by zero.

Insight cards: dropped HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW badge + `{insight.confidence}%
confidence` (hardcoded constant on every insight = vibes-by-numbers).
Sage-tinted left-border replaces the red/yellow/blue heatmap.
